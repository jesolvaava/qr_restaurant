About QR Restaurant

At QR Restaurant, we revolutionize the dining experience by eliminating the need for waiters. Each table is equipped with a unique QR code that customers can scan to access our digital menu. From there, they can browse our delicious offerings, add items to their cart, and place orders directly from their phones. Once the order is placed, a notification is sent to the kitchen, ensuring quick and efficient service. After enjoying their meal, customers can mark their order as finished and receive their bill directly on their device, with options to pay online or in person. Experience the future of dining with QR Restaurant!
